1. Introduction

Calculus helps describe change. Its core operations—derivatives and integrals—allow us to model motion, growth, and accumulation.

2. Power Series

A geometric series example:

\[ \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^n = 1 \]

Such convergence is fundamental in both mathematical analysis and physical modeling.
